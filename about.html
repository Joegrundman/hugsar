--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## HUGSAR Jo Case, Tom Grundman, Matt Dicken and Jonathan Green are HUGSAR, a Bristol and London based four piece. We mix proggy grooves with psychedelic pads and hooky melodies. The imminent release of our debut "Cake" E.P. is eagerly anticipated by everyone that knows about it...
HUGSAR also have a great live show and a dedicated fanbase
